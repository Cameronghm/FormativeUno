What are the uses of machine learning?

Where is machine learning found?

Machine learning systems are used all around us. For example, they are used to recommend which product you might want to buy next on Amazon or video you want to may want to watch on Netflix.

Also, every Google search uses multiple machine-learning systems, to understand the language in your query and personalise your results - so fishing enthusiasts searching for "bass" aren't inundated with results about guitars!

Future Machine Learning Uses

Beyond the visible manifestations of machine learning, systems are starting to find a use in just about every industry.

  • Computer Vision for driverless cars
  • Drones and Delivery robots
  • Speech and Language and synthesis for chatbots and service robots
  • Facial Recognition for surveillance in countries like China
  • Aiding researchers in spotting genetic sequences related to diseases
  • Fraud detection

Deep-learning could eventually pave the way for robots that could learn directly from humans. Researchers at Nvidia have been creating a deep-learning system designed to teach a robot to carry out a task simply by observing that job being performed by a human.
